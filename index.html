About

Equal parts design and code.

I'm Maya — I spend my days moving between Figma and a terminal, usually on the same project. Ten years in, I still think the best interfaces disappear: no excess motion, no decoration that isn't pulling weight, just clarity that gets out of the way. When I'm not working I'm usually re-grouting something in my apartment that didn't need it.
